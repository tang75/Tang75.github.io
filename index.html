Methodology
For each medication phase, statistics are computed from the last N readings OR minimum phase window (whichever is longer) before the next medication change, capturing the effect of that regimen at steady state. If fewer than N readings exist within the lookback window, all available readings are used. The final (current) row reports all readings within the recent window. The same recent window is used for the KPI dashboard on the Timeline tab. Δ columns show the change from the prior phase to help track treatment response. All parameters are configurable in the settings bar at the top of the page.
