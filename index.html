Methodology & Parameters
For each medication phase, statistics are computed from the last N readings before the next medication change, capturing the effect of that regimen at steady state. If fewer than N readings exist within the lookback window, all available readings are used. The final row reports the most recent readings within a configurable time window. Δ columns show the change from the prior phase to help track treatment response.
